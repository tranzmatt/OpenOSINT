## What requires a commercial license?

A commercial license is required if **all** of the following are true:
- You are using OpenOSINT (or a derivative) as part of a product or service
- That product or service is closed-source (proprietary)
- You are generating revenue from it (directly or indirectly)

**Does NOT require a commercial license:**
- Personal projects
- Academic or research use
- Open source projects (MIT-compatible)
- Internal tooling at non-commercial organizations
